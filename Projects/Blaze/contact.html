North (Route 1 South)

From Route 1 south take the MA-129 E exit towards Lynn. Continue straight for about 0.3 miles. Then turn left onto Salem St (signs for Lynn/Swampscott) and travel about 0.7 miles. At the traffic circle, continue straight onto MA-129 E for 3.1 miles and continue onto Chestnut St for 0.4 miles. Turn right onto Western Ave, travel 0.3 miles and Stadium will be on the right.

West (95/128)

From route 95 take exit 44B for MA-129 E. At the traffic circle, take the 2nd exit onto MA-129 E for 3.2 miles and continue onto Chestnut St for 0.4 miles. Turn right onto Western Ave, travel 0.3 miles and Stadium will be on the right.

South (Route 1 North/Boston)

From Route 1 North take the MA-60 exit toward Malden/Revere. At the traffic circle, take the 1st exit onto MA-60 E/Squire Rd heading to Revere for 0.9 miles. At the next traffic circle, take the 4th exit onto MA-107 N/Salem Turnpike heading to Lynn/Salem, Continue to follow MA-107 N, Destination will be on the left in 4.6 miles.
