Brochure Site

When a business card site just won't do, consider our brochure site package. While not as extensive as our Basic Business Package, the Brochure site provides a showcase for you and your business at a reasonable price point.

More than just your contact information, the Brochure site gives your visitors a clear idea of what your business is about. This is the chance to introduce visitors to who you are and what you do.

With up to six pages to spread your content across, the Brochure site gives you space to show examples of your work, share testimonials from your clients, publish current specials or news.

The site will still contain everything found in the Business Card package, along with additional detailed information. However, it will be organized in a clearly designed and easy to navigate site.
