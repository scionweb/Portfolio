Design Packages

Not every business has the same needs when it comes to a presence on the internet. Some just need a simple way of letting the world know they exist, while others require many pages of information. Wherever your business may fall along the spectrum, Scion Web Design can build you a site that is a perfect fit.


$195

A Business Card Website is a one-page announcement to the World Wide Web that you exist. It is inexpensive, easily maintained, and provides information about you or your business, contact details, links to other social profiles (think FaceBook, Twitter, etc.) Business card websites take on many forms. Like actual business cards, while many are very straightforward, nothing says you can't get a little creative.

$495

A Brochure Website provides a showcase of you or your business, along with all of the information you would expect to find: contact information, goods and services, pricing, location. Like a real brochure, the goal of this type of website is to give visitors a clear picture of who you are and what you do, in a few clear and easy to navigate pages.

$895

Our Basic Business Website takes the concept of the brochure site and builds upon it, adding more information about you or your business. While for many small businesses a brochure site may be sufficient, some may require additional pages to provide visitors a complete and thorough understanding of who they are and what they do.
