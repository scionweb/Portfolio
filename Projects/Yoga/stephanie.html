Stephanie McQuillan

Stephanie's path to yoga started in 1999 when she took her first vinyasa class. She loved everything about it; the workout, the music, the environment, the sense of peace that permeated the room. Then, like usual, "life's schedule" got in the way, and she fell off the "path to yoga."

But after some time passed and a new routine formed she found her way back to the yogic path, realizing that once you've experienced yoga, it doesn't really leave you. After years of practice she found that she really needed to know more; more about each pose, the breath, and certainly more about the spirit of yoga. So, she became certified at the 200-hour level by Kim Valeri of yogaspirit Studios. She is now a Registered Yoga Teacher (RYT) in Iyengar-style.

Stephanie's classes focus on connecting movement with breath in a flowing sequence, all the while really focusing on alignment. She aims to strengthen mind, body and spirit in a light and energetic atmosphere.
