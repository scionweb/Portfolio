Our Studio

Our second floor studio is bright and open, and comfortably accomodates twelve students. The room has heat and air conditioning, along with two sky lights and a large picture window at one end.


While many of our regular students have their own mats, we do have extra mats available to those who need them. We also can provide yoga blocks, straps, and meditation cushions when necessary.


If you are a yoga instructor and would like to teach your own class, our studio is available for rent, and our rates are extremely affordable. We can provide all of the above equipment, along with blankets, eye pillows, tennis balls, and a CD player.
