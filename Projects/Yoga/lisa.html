Lisa Bukkhegyi

Lisa's practice of yoga is an active practice of compassion. She began practicing yoga as part of her own healing from treatments of a malignant brain tumor. Yoga was a vital part of her own recovery.

Now, thirteen years cancer free and ten years into her yoga practice, Lisa brings a compassionate heart to others as a mother of two teenage daughters, Hospice Volunteer and mentor with Imerman Angels, a one to one network for people with cancer. Her work in these organizations has brought an awareness to the emotional, physical and spiritual needs of others.

She is certified by Kim Valeri of Yogaspirit Studios and is registered with Yoga Alliance (RYT). Drawing on the Blessings in her life, she cultivates feelings of gratitude and contentment for her own well being.

Lisa continues to develop her own practice through attending workshops and classes broadening her knowledge and understanding of yoga as a lifestyle in which to maintain balance in the body, mind and spirit.
