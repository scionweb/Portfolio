Essex (n) - A lovely town on the seacoast of Massachusetts. Home of Woodman's, Essex Yoga Coop, and some of the loveliest people on earth.
Yoga (n) - a Hindu theistic philosophy teaching the suppression of all activity of body, mind, and will in order that the self may realize its distinction from them and attain liberation.
Coop (n) - Shortened form of "cooperative." A collection of individuals providing a product or service to the public, as in a farmer's cooperative.

We are a cooperative of Yoga practitioners who operate out of a lovely space in Essex bringing all the gifts of Yoga including good health, peaceful minds, and strong spirits to the good people of Essex County and beyond.


We feel that this is an awesome way to run a Yoga studio, in that it recognizes that the true wealth of Yoga is in the teachings and the community.
