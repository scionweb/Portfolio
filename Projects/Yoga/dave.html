Instructors

Dave Lind

Dave is a Professional Level Kripalu Yoga Teacher at the 500-hour level. He has an eclectic style drawing from YogaSpirit Studios principles of alignment, Kripalu heart-centered focus, and a developing love of the Anusara style of John Friend. Expect the classes to be challenging yet heart centered with a bit of humor.

Dave also offers Private Sessions at the Coop as well as out of his home.
