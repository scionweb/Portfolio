How To Find Us

Our studio is located at 121 Eastern Avenue in Essex, MA, which is also Route 133. From Route 128 take exit 14, heading west onto 133. Just 1/2 mile or so past the Gloucester-Essex line you will find us on the left side of the road (coming from 128), on the second floor above Schooners Market.
